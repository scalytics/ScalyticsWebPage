Explore the capabilities that made Scalytics incomparable

Smart Job Splitting

Internal tasks in a single job can take advantages from different cloud providers.

Scalytics considers each sub-task as an element for optimization. This process always takes into account the data movement costs that will be incurred by applying multi-cloud processing.

Platform Independence

Scalytics exposes its own operators, each of this operators can run on multiple cloud providers and big data platforms.

The user no longer needs to be aware or to re-implement their applications to use a different platform. Whenever an alternative platform gets better performance or lowers its costs to perform a particular task. Scalytics will notice this opportunity and proceed to replace the platform without any effort.
